<p><p>Pipeline separators are devices used to separate different phases of materials in pipelines, primarily in the oil and gas industry. The centrifugal separator uses centrifugal force to efficiently separate fluids of different densities, effectively removing solids and water from hydrocarbons. Filter separators employ a filtration system to capture particulates and separate liquids, ensuring only clean fluids pass through. Both types are crucial for maintaining product quality and operational efficiency in pipeline transport systems.</p></p>
