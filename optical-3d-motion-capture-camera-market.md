<p><p>The Optical 3D Motion Capture Camera Market is divided into two main types: Marker Points and Non-Marker Points. Marker Points systems utilize reflective markers affixed to the subject, which cameras track to capture precise motion data. This method is commonly used in film and gaming for high accuracy. In contrast, Non-Marker Points systems rely on advanced algorithms to detect and track motion without physical markers, offering more flexibility and ease of setup, making them suitable for applications in virtual reality and live performances.</p></p>
