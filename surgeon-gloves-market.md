<p><p>The surgeon gloves market is categorized into four main types: latex, vinyl, nitrile rubber, and neoprene. Latex gloves offer high elasticity and comfort but can cause allergic reactions. Vinyl gloves are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk procedures but lack durability. Nitrile rubber gloves are chemical-resistant and provide excellent protection, making them popular in various medical settings. Neoprene gloves combine durability with flexibility and are suitable for various applications, including those requiring chemical resistance, appealing to diverse healthcare needs.</p></p>
