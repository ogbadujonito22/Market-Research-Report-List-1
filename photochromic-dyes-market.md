<p><p>The photochromic dyes market encompasses various types, each characterized by unique properties and applications. Azobenzenes undergo reversible structural changes under light, making them useful in smart materials. Spiropyrans display significant color changes in response to UV light, ideal for sensors. Diarylethenes are notable for their thermal stability and are often used in optical devices. Fulgides provide rapid switching capabilities, while Hexaarylbiimidazole offers high photosensitivity. Spiroperimidines are emerging for their potential in advanced photonic applications, expanding the market's versatility.</p></p>
