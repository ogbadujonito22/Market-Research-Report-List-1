<p><p>Column spot welders are categorized mainly into single-phase and three-phase markets based on their power supply and operational efficiency. Single-phase welders, typically suited for light-duty applications, operate on a single electrical circuit and are popular in small workshops. In contrast, three-phase welders, designed for heavy-duty industrial applications, offer higher power output, allowing for efficient welding of thicker materials and continuous operation. This differentiation caters to varying operational needs across different industries and production scales.</p></p>
