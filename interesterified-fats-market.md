<p><p>The interesterified fats market is categorized into two main types: chemical interesterified fats and enzymatic interesterified fats. Chemical interesterification involves the use of chemical catalysts to rearrange fatty acids in triglycerides, enhancing properties like melting point and stability. In contrast, enzymatic interesterification utilizes specific enzymes to achieve similar modifications with greater precision and at lower temperatures, resulting in healthier profiles and improved functional characteristics. Both types are widely utilized in food formulations and margarine production, contributing to diverse applications in the food industry.</p></p>
