<p><strong>The Airport Cargo Conveyors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Type</li><li>Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airport cargo conveyors market is primarily categorized into two types: belt type and roller type conveyors. Belt type conveyors utilize a continuous loop of material to transport cargo, offering smooth and reliable movement, ideal for various cargo sizes. In contrast, roller type conveyors use a series of rollers to maximize efficiency and facilitate the handling of heavier loads, making them suitable for airport environments with diverse cargo types. Both types enhance operational efficiency in cargo handling and logistics.</p></p>
