<p><p>The semiconductor process valves market includes various types designed for precision fluid control in manufacturing environments. Diaphragm valves utilize a flexible membrane for tight sealing, while bellows valves feature a metal bellows for leak-tight performance. Vacuum valves manage low-pressure systems, and ball valves offer quick shut-off capabilities. Butterfly valves enable rapid flow regulation, whereas gate valves provide on-off flow control. Angle valves facilitate flow direction changes, and Teflon valves offer chemical resistance. Other specialized valves cater to unique application requirements.</p></p>
