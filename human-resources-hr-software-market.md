<p><p>Human Resources (HR) software is categorized into three main types: on-premises, cloud-based, and web-based. On-premises software requires local installation and offers extensive control over data but demands higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts. Cloud-based solutions are hosted online, providing flexibility, scalability, and ease of access while typically operating on a subscription model. Web-based HR software operates through a browser interface, offering similar benefits to cloud solutions but may be limited in functionality depending on internet connectivity. Each type caters to diverse organizational needs.</p></p>
