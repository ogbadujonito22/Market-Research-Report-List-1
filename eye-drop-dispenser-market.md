<p><p>The eye drop dispenser market is categorized into multiple dose dispensers and single dose dispensers. Multiple dose dispensers are designed to deliver several applications from a single bottle, ensuring consistent dosages while maintaining sterility. They often feature mechanisms that minimize contamination. In contrast, single dose dispensers are prefilled with a single application, providing maximum hygiene and reducing the risk of drug degradation. Both types cater to different patient needs, offering convenience and reliability in ocular medication delivery.</p></p>
