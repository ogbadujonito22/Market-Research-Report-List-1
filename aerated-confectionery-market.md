<p><p>The aerated confectionery market is categorized into three types based on the level of aeration. High aerated confectionery features a light, fluffy texture with significant air incorporation, resulting in products like marshmallows and aerated chocolates. Medium aerated confectionery has a balanced texture, offering a moderate level of aeration, seen in items like soft caramels. Low aerated confectionery presents a denser mouthfeel, typically found in hard candies and gummies, where minimal air is incorporated, providing a chewier experience.</p></p>
