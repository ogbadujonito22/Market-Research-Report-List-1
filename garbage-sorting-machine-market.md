<p><p>The garbage sorting machine market is primarily categorized into three types: CCD Vision, Laser Vision, and Others. CCD Vision technology utilizes charge-coupled device sensors for effective image analysis, enabling precise identification and sorting of waste materials based on color and texture. Laser Vision systems employ laser beams to detect and classify various waste types based on their optical characteristics, enhancing sorting accuracy. The "Others" category encompasses alternative sorting technologies, including infrared sorting and mechanical methods, catering to diverse waste management needs.</p></p>
