<p><p>The premature infant incubator market consists of two primary types: transport infant incubators and normal infant incubators. Transport infant incubators are designed for safe and controlled transport of premature infants, ensuring temperature regulation and stability during transit. In contrast, normal infant incubators are stationary units used in neonatal care units, providing a controlled environment that mimics the womb to support the growth and development of premature infants. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing infant survival rates and improving health outcomes.</p></p>
