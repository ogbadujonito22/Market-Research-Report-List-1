<p><p>The Metal Cutting Machine (MCM) market encompasses various technologies tailored for precision metal fabrication. Laser cutting machines utilize focused laser beams for accuracy and intricate shapes. Waterjet cutting machines employ high-pressure water mixed with abrasive materials to slice through metals without thermal distortion. Plasma cutting machines use ionized gas to cut through electrically conductive materials, while flame cutting machines use a high-temperature flame for thicker materials. Other methods may include mechanical cutting or advanced hybrid technologies, expanding the market’s scope.</p></p>
