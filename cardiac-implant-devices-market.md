<p><p>The cardiac implant devices market encompasses several key types that enhance heart function and manage cardiovascular diseases. Implantable Cardiac Pacemakers (ICPs) regulate heartbeat rhythms. Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs) deliver shocks to correct life-threatening arrhythmias. Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Devices (CRTs) coordinate heart contractions for patients with heart failure. Coronary Stents are used to open blocked arteries, improving blood flow. Additionally, the "Others" category includes a range of emerging devices and technologies aimed at treating various cardiac conditions.</p></p>
