<p><p>The Single-Phase Smart Meter market is divided into two primary types: Direct Current (DC) Meters and Alternating Current (AC) Meters. DC Meters are designed to measure direct current, commonly used in renewable energy applications such as solar power systems. In contrast, AC Meters are utilized for conventional energy supply, measuring alternating current typically found in residential and commercial electric services. Both types enhance energy monitoring and management, enabling consumers to optimize usage and utilities to improve service efficiency.</p></p>
