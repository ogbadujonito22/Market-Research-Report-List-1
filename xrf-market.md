<p><p>The XRF market is divided into two primary types: Energy Dispersive X-ray Fluorescence (EDXRF) and Wavelength Dispersive X-ray Fluorescence (WDXRF). EDXRF offers rapid analysis and is ideal for multi-element detection, making it suitable for various applications like environmental monitoring and metals analysis. In contrast, WDXRF provides higher resolution and accuracy, making it preferable for advanced quantitative analysis in industries such as petrochemicals and materials science. Each type serves distinct analytical needs across diverse sectors.</p></p>
