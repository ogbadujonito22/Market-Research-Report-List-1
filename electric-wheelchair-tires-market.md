<p><strong>The Electric Wheelchair Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic/Air Filled Wheelchair Tires</li><li>Solid Wheelchair Tires</li><li>Foam Filled Wheelchair Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric wheelchair tires market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Pneumatic/Air Filled Tires** offer excellent shock absorption and smooth rides but require regular maintenance to avoid punctures. </p><p>2. **Solid Tires** are durable and maintenance-free, providing increased stability and puncture resistance but can result in a harsher ride on uneven surfaces. </p><p>3. **Foam Filled Tires** combine benefits of both, being puncture-proof and offering cushioning, but may not match the performance of pneumatic tires in terms of ride comfort.</p></p>
