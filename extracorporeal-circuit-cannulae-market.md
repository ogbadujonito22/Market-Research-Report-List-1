<p><p>The extracorporeal circuit cannulae market primarily includes arterial and venous cannulae, essential for procedures like cardiopulmonary bypass and dialysis. Arterial cannulae are designed to access the arterial system, allowing for blood return to the body, while venous cannulae facilitate blood removal from the venous system to be processed externally. Both types are crucial in managing blood flow during surgeries and treatments, ensuring patient safety and effective performance of extracorporeal circulation systems in various medical applications.</p></p>
