<p><strong>The Turntable Cartridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MM</li><li>MI</li><li>MC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The turntable cartridge market is primarily divided into three types: Moving Magnet (MM), Moving Iron (MI), and Moving Coil (MC). MM cartridges utilize a magnet attached to a stylus, providing good sound quality and are more affordable, making them popular among beginners. MI cartridges, though less common, offer a balance between MM and MC, with decent sound and lower output impedance. MC cartridges feature a lightweight coil and advanced design, delivering superior audio quality, preferred by audiophiles and professionals despite their higher cost.</p></p>
