<p><p>The remote monitoring equipment market is divided into two main types: external and implantable devices. External remote monitoring equipment includes wearable devices like smartwatches and fitness trackers, which collect health data without invasive procedures, allowing continuous patient monitoring. In contrast, implantable remote monitoring equipment consists of devices surgically placed inside the body, such as cardiac monitors and glucose sensors, providing real-time health data directly from within the patient's body, enhancing treatment and management of chronic conditions.</p></p>
