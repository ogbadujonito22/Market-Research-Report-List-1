<p><p>The specimen retrieval nets market can be categorized into detachable and non-detachable types. Detachable specimen retrieval nets allow clinicians to easily detach the net from the retrieval device after capturing the specimen, enhancing flexibility and ease of use during procedures. In contrast, non-detachable specimen retrieval nets remain fixed to the retrieval device, providing stability and reducing the risk of specimen loss during retrieval. Both types are essential in minimally invasive surgeries for safely capturing various biological specimens.</p></p>
