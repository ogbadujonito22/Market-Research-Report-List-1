<p><p>The Electronic Bionic Eye market comprises two primary types: external eyes and implanted eyes. In contrast, implanted eyes involve surgical procedures to place electronic components directly within the eye, interfacing with the optic nerve to restore vision. Both types aim to improve the quality of life for those with visual impairments, leveraging technology for innovative solutions.</p></p>
