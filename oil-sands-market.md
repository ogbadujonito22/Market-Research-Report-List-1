<p><ul><li>Oil Sands Exploitation</li><li>Oil Sands Isolation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil sands market comprises two main types: oil sands exploitation and oil sands isolation. Oil sands exploitation involves the extraction of bitumen from natural deposits using techniques such as surface mining and in-situ methods, aiming to convert bitumen into synthetic crude oil. In contrast, oil sands isolation focuses on the separation and processing of bitumen from other materials within the sands, ensuring efficient recovery and minimizing environmental impact. Together, they serve the growing demand for energy while addressing sustainability concerns.</p></p>
