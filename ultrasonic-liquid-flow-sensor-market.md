<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Liquid Flow Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline Type</li><li>Clamp-On Type</li><li>Insertion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic liquid flow sensor market comprises three main types: inline, clamp-on, and insertion. Inline type sensors are integrated directly into the pipeline, offering accurate flow measurements and minimal pressure loss. Clamp-on type sensors, which are mounted externally, provide a non-invasive solution for flow measurement without interrupting the system, making them ideal for sensitive applications. Insertion type sensors, partially immersed in the liquid, balance accuracy and installation flexibility, catering to varying pipe sizes and configurations in diverse industrial settings.</p></p>
