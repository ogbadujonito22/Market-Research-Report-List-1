<p><p>The Meat Protein Analysis Equipment market primarily includes DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared) technology. DEXA technology utilizes X-ray beams to accurately measure bone and soft tissue composition, enabling precise protein content analysis in meat. In contrast, NIR technology employs light reflection to assess the chemical composition of meat, including protein levels, fat content, and moisture. Both technologies offer efficient, non-destructive methods for ensuring quality control and nutritional assessment in the meat industry.</p></p>
