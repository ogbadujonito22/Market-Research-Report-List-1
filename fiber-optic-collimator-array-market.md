<p><p>The fiber optic collimator array market is primarily categorized into two types: one-dimensional (1D) and two-dimensional (2D) fiber collimator arrays. The 1D fiber collimator array focuses on linear configurations, effectively collimating light from multiple fibers into a single axis, suitable for applications like sensors and telecommunications. In contrast, the 2D fiber collimator array enables light collimation across two dimensions, allowing for more complex arrangements and improved performance in applications requiring greater spatial precision, such as imaging and laser systems.</p></p>
