<p><ul><li>Life/Family Takaful</li><li>General Takaful</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Takaful operates through two main market types: Life/Family Takaful and General Takaful. Life/Family Takaful focuses on providing financial protection and savings for individuals and families, covering risks such as death, disability, and critical illness. In contrast, General Takaful encompasses a broader range of insurance products, addressing risks related to property, liability, and health. Both types adhere to Islamic principles, promoting mutual cooperation and shared responsibility among participants while avoiding elements of conventional insurance that conflict with Shariah law.</p></p>
